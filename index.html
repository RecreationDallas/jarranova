why jarranova

Technology changes rapidly. Human behavior changes slowly. Sustainable growth requires understanding both.

Jarranova was founded on a simple belief: the organizations that thrive in the future will be those that understand people better than their competitors.

Technology continues to reshape how businesses operate, how brands engage, and how decisions are made. Yet the fundamental drivers of human behavior – trust, relevance, belonging, value, and loyalty – remain remarkably consistent. Sustainable growth requires leaders who can navigate both.

We partner with Founders, CEOs, and Boards at pivotal moments: when growth has stalled, a brand needs renewed relevance, a business is preparing for its next chapter, or a leadership team must align around a bold new direction.

Drawing inspiration from the brilliance of a nova, Jarranova helps leaders see what others miss, ask better questions, and move forward with greater clarity and conviction. Our work combines strategic rigor, creative intuition, and the executional judgment that comes from having lived it—helping organizations build enduring value in a world of constant change.

Marissa Jarratt

Founder & principal advisor

Recognized by Forbes for four consecutive years as one of the world’s most influential and entrepreneurial CMOs. Former EVP and Chief Marketing & Sustainability Officer at 7-Eleven. Builder of brands, businesses, and customer ecosystems that earn lasting loyalty.

Through Jarranova, Marissa advises CEOs, leadership teams, and boards on the decisions that shape enterprise growth, commercial transformation, and modern marketing. She currently serves as an Independent Director at HUGE, a design and technology company, at 7-Eleven Mexico, a convenience retailer with approximately 2,000 locations, and at KERA Public Media.
